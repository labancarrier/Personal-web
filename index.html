My Story

As a young child, I’ve always known that there was a higher power, a creator. I grew up knowing Jesus was my friend and he died on the cross but one day I understood that he didn’t die for just some reason. He suffered it all because he loved me. Ever since then, since I really understood how much he loves me, I’ve tried to share that love. My love will never compare to his but I strive to walk in his footsteps.

My Ministry

My life is ministry and my ministry is life. In God’s holy word, Jesus tells us “Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another. By this everyone will know that you are my disciples if you love one another.” With this, my life’s goal is to love the unlovable, to comfort the uncomforted, to seek and love on the lost sheep.
