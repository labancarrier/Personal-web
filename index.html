My Story

Mele Kalikimaka is the thing to say on a bright Hawaiian Christmas Day, but what do they say in Thailand? Well, they would say “Sukhsant wan khristmas” but if you are like me you can’t read it let alone pronounce it. But have no worries, chances are you are not in Thailand and probably don’t ever plan to be in Thailand for Christmas. That’s alright I never planned to be in Thailand for Christmas either but God had other plans. Anyways, I wanna wish you a Merry Christmas from Thailand! I pray that God fills you and your family full of joy this season. I pray he continues to bless you beyond belief.
